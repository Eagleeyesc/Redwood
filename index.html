How our data-driven service actually works

+EV Betting

We find you bets where you have a mathematical edge against the sportsbooks

Redwood finds mistakes in betting markets and gives you the 'EV' line before the bookmakers can react. These are bets where the chances of winning are higher than they should be based on a mix of data modelling and consensus odds.

You place the bets. We tell you the exact sportsbook, game, market, players, and recommended stake size. You make the bet on the book (FanDuel, DraftKings, etc). Sportsbooks move their lines quickly, so you need to place these data-driven bets before they disappear. Overtime, your bankroll will grow, we guarantee it.

How do I sign up?

Press 'Buy Now' in the pricing tab above and you'll be taken to our bot to proceed with payment, you'll then be added to the group automatically.

What sports do you cover?

We currently cover the NFL, NBA, College football and basketball, NHL and MLB (when in season) there may also from time to time be picks from misc sports if we find +EV but those are the main focuses.

What bookmakers do you currently place bets on?

Our data allows us to place bets on all leading North American sportsbooks. Fanduel, Draftkings, BetMGM, Caesars etc. We're currently at 22 bookmakers with more to hopefully be added soon.

Is there a minimum bankroll required?

To get the most out of the service we would recommend your bankroll is atleast $2000+, but there's no specific minimum required in order to get started, feel free to contact us to discuss what works for you.

How do the picks get delivered?

We deliver our picks via Telegram, you'll be added to the private group once payment is recieved. Please enable notifications so you can stake ASAP before there's a movement in odds.

Are you affiliated with any sportsbooks?

We are not paid by or affiliated with any sportsbook. Our goal is to take money away from them and share it with you.

is this service legal and reputable?

All of this is completely legal. Tax/VAT is paid on all transactions by us and we're a registered company.

What payment methods are accepted?

All subscription payments are handled by Stripe who are a well known and trusted payment processor. We accept all major card providers such as Visa, Mastercard, Amex etc. We are working on implementing Skrill & Paypal payments in the future.

Can I cancel anytime?

You can cancel anytime and your subscription will finish at the end of that current billing period.

Can I get a refund for my purchase?

You are unable to recieve a refund as you are added to the private group immediately upon payment. However if you have a month that isn't profitable, we will give you a free month on the house.
